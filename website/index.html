WHAT THE APP DOES TODAY

Modular workforce & field operations

Companies enable modules they need — ticketing, scheduling, payroll, paperless, compliance, contractors, property management, asset compliance, and external reporting — without maintaining separate products.

📋Jobs & job cards

Create and track work in the field with photos, signatures, costs, and status — tied to clients and sites.

📅Scheduling & shifts

Plan recurring shifts and assignments so crews know where to be and HR sees coverage.

🕒Attendance & punches

Clock in/out with location-backed punches; HR dashboards for attendance and attendance exports.

💰Payroll & payment approvals

Salary and hourly models, payment approval flows, and visibility into what’s owed versus paid.

📄Paperless ops

Form templates, submissions, and signatures — replace loose paperwork with an audit-friendly trail.

🏢Property management

Sites, units, and residents for estates and managing-agent workflows — alongside jobs and assets.

🔧Assets & compliance

Asset register with inspections and certificates — geysers, lifts, fire panels, and similar registers.

🤝Contractors & workers

Employees, contractors, and subcontractors in one worker model; invites and multi-company access for providers.

📈Reporting & BI hooks

SQL views for enriched jobs, maintenance, costs, contractors, and residents — connect Power BI or Metabase directly.
