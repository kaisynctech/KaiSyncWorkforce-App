Production modules

Every KaiSync Workforce capability, explained

Sixteen first-class modules in production — from GPS attendance and field jobs through payroll, finance, property, and external portals. Enable only what your company needs; each module shares the same company scope and data contracts.

01

Attendance

Clock in and out with a timestamp and GPS fix on every punch. HR validates punches against shifts and assignments so out-of-window sign-ins are flagged before they reach payroll.

  • GPS punches with optional reverse-geocoded address lines for HR review
  • Shift validation and site or branch radius rules where your policy requires them
  • Attendance sessions and history for approvals, disputes, and payroll prep
  • Exports aligned to your pay run and audit workflows
02

Jobs

Operational job cards for field execution — status, assignment, checklists, costs, and evidence on one record tied to clients, sites, and crews.

  • Job cards with assignment, status workflow, and site linkage
  • Checklists, signatures, photos, and cost lines captured in the field
  • Feeds attendance rules when jobs define where work must happen
  • Execution data rolls into projects, inventory, and reporting
03

Projects

Track client deals and delivery at project level — linked jobs, milestones, and commercial context so operations and finance agree on what was sold versus what was done.

  • Project records linked to client accounts and contracted scope
  • Jobs roll up under projects for progress and cost visibility
  • Foundation for client portal updates and account-level reporting
  • Cleaner handoffs between sales, dispatch, and field teams
04

Payroll

Run pay with payslips, approvals, statutory deductions, and YTD totals — built to consume clean attendance and leave signals instead of shadow spreadsheets.

  • Payslip generation and employee self-service views
  • Approval flows before release and payment
  • Statutory lines and year-to-date balances on each record
  • Overtime and exception signals surfaced before the pay run closes
05

Finance

Company finance in the same tenant — invoices, VAT, cashflow visibility, and approval workflows with an audit trail aligned to how you sign off in real life.

  • Invoicing and receivables tied to clients and projects
  • VAT-aware lines where your jurisdiction requires them
  • Cashflow and outstanding balances for finance leads
  • Approval steps and audit history on financial actions
06

Leave

Employees apply; managers approve — with balances and history that stay payroll-ready instead of buried in email threads.

  • Leave applications and manager approvals in one system of record
  • Policy and balance visibility for the right roles
  • Approved leave flows into payroll without manual re-entry
  • Exports compatible with your existing pay processes
07

Incidents

Report, assign, and close incidents with comments, status history, and escalation — structured evidence instead of loose photos in chat.

  • Employee and HR incident reporting with categorisation
  • Threaded comments and assignee workflow on each record
  • Status history for audits and client or regulator requests
  • Links to jobs, sites, and property context where relevant
08

Inventory

Stock register, usage, and allocations tied to the jobs and sites that actually consume materials — less “where did that kit go?” across vans and depots.

  • Parts and stock lines with quantity and location traceability
  • Usage logged against work orders for cost control
  • Allocations from depot to field crews where you need accountability
  • Supports field services and facilities workflows
09

Suppliers

A supplier register with contact details, linked invoices, and procurement context — finance and operations aligned on who you bought from and what you owe.

  • Supplier profiles with banking and contact fields
  • Invoice capture and linkage to finance workflows
  • Visibility for AP and operations without duplicate spreadsheets
  • Supports inventory replenishment and job cost lines
10

Contractors

Manage contractor firms with scoped access — invite partners, assign jobs they can see, and keep client data inside your tenant boundaries.

  • Contractor firm records with invite and onboarding flows
  • Dedicated contractor portal surface for scoped job visibility
  • Jobs and performance signals without over-sharing internal data
  • Cleaner handoffs between in-house crews and external vendors
11

Messaging

In-app threads between HR and employees — company-scoped comms tied to the platform instead of scattered personal messaging apps.

  • Threaded conversations with the right visibility per role
  • Operational instructions stay beside jobs and attendance
  • Audit-friendly compared to unmanaged group chats
  • Works across HR dashboards and employee workspaces
12

Property Management

Model portfolios as sites → units → residents — built for estates and managing-agent rolls where repeat issues and per-unit reporting matter.

  • Site hierarchy with units and resident records
  • Jobs and incidents linked to the property they affect
  • Better contractor briefings with full estate context
  • Scales from a handful of blocks to larger portfolios
13

Reports & Analytics

Operational and executive views over the same data your teams capture — exports, KPIs, and platform reports without one-off CSV archaeology before every meeting.

  • Roll-ups over jobs, attendance, costs, and property signals
  • KPI panels for leadership and finance reviews
  • Export-friendly outputs for BI and spreadsheet workflows
  • Platform-level reporting for multi-company operators
14

My PA

A built-in personal assistant workspace for tasks, reminders, and follow-ups — with optional calendar sync hooks so commitments stay aligned with the workday.

  • Tasks with due dates and reminder nudges
  • Patterns for meetings, callbacks, and operational follow-ups
  • Calendar sync hooks for Google and Microsoft where enabled
  • Lives beside jobs, leave, and messaging in one app
15

Client Portal

External clients sign in with a client code — scoped visibility into their projects, progress updates, and messaging without access to your full tenant.

  • Code-based login separate from HR email authentication
  • Project and job progress shared on your terms
  • In-app messaging with your team on permitted threads
  • Reduces status-update email noise for account managers
16

Contractor Portal

Contractor firms authenticate with a contractor code — see assigned jobs, log site visits, and execute scoped work without inheriting HR or client data they should not touch.

  • Code-based login for external firm users
  • Site visits and job execution on assigned work only
  • Checklists, evidence, and status updates from the field
  • Aligned with the Contractors module and job assignment rules

Enable the modules you need — we will help you map KaiSync Workforce to your rollout.
