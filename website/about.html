About KaiSync Workforce

Built for real operations, not generic task lists

KaiSync Workforce is a multi-tenant Flutter app backed by Supabase: jobs, scheduling, punches, payroll and approvals, paperless forms, property layers, assets, contractors, and BI-ready views — switched on per company.

Our mission

Give every services team and managing agent one operating system for field execution, HR visibility, and structured data that plugs into the tools you already use.
